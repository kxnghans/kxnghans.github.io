Intro

In my short life I have done things beyond my expectation. As a boy growing up in the suburbs of Ghana, West Africa, life was never rosy but I always believed that exploration was the key to me having a bright future.

Through the course of my education, I discovered so many aptitudes I never dreamt of having and this profoundly convinced me that there was much more to be discovered. Of these I took the opportunity to develop and nurture my artistic skills, learnt how to play the piano and my passion for soccer further motivated me to play for the school's varsity soccer team. In addition, I actively participated in clubs and societies at school build each other professionally, in effect pushing my boundaries for success. These sparked the burning desire in me that helped me realize that Together Everyone Achieves More.

I fell in love with technology and how easily it can dramatically change the outlook of someone's life. Whether I am the beneficiary, or creating platforms to pool societies skills together to shine, this is just the beginning of my journey!
